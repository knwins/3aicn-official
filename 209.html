In general, when the fire broke out, people tend to think of using fire extinguishers, spray equipment to extinguish the fire, in fact, are "passive fire"; in before the fire, in buildings of the delay or even prevent the spread of fire fireproof material, you can achieve change "passive fire" to "active fire," a greater degree of reduction of fire losses.

3M's invention of innovative passive fire protection systems for over 40 years of history. In fact, 3M is the world's only company able to produce a large number of passive protection material certified company. 3M fire blocking material, using the world's most advanced thermal expansion, heat, insulation, and apply technology, outsourcing, energy efficiency prevent the flames, smoke and toxic gases spread and spread. These materials include: sealing material through the holes in fire protection, structural joints (wall joints, curtain wall around the joints, etc.) fire sealing materials, steel and emergency fire protection materials, electrical circuits, smoke pipe / duct / booster plastic pipe fire protection materials and fire doors fire seal and so on.

3M passive fire protection systems are used around the world, and by the following authorities such as UL (Underwriters Laboratories), FM (U.S. insurance companies, research centers) Warnock-Hersey, Omega Point and the Chinese National Fire Protection Material Testing Center and other institutions that qualified for the application of a wide range of passive fire protection systems.

Currently, 3M advocated "passive fire protection" concept has been widely accepted around the world, and always protect life and property safety. In China, 2009, the Ministry of Public Security has issued new regulations in the fire, requiring the construction of public spaces to be used more passive fire protection measures, and new fire-retardant materials. This is a passive fire protection technology to a greater extent to promote the effective protection.
