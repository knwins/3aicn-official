Fire blocking materials, the latest standards

Fire blocking materials, the latest standards, Standardization Administration of the People's Republic of industry self-released announcement No. 147, approved and promulgated GB 23864-2009 "fire blocking material."

Been associated with companies around the action immediately, preparing to cope with the new standards to meet market demand. www.3aicn.com

The latest approved release of GB 23864-2009 "fire blocking material" standard, mandatory national standards, in 2010

February 1 onwards. Public safety industry standard GA 161-1997 "fire blocking material performance requirements and test methods

"With the new national standards will be implemented and abolished. www.3aicn.com

Fire blocking material is mainly used for sealing building fire district through the holes and cracks to ensure that fires the flame and high

Flue gas temperature will not spread to another fire district. Enacted in 1997 GA 161-1997 "fire blocking material performance requirements

And test methods "industry standard specifications at the time of the production of such products has played a positive role, but also in the many years of use

Found that the standard there are many problems and limitations, as compared with foreign advanced standards there is a big gap, can not meet the technical progress

Supervision of fire protection products and the need for the urgent need for a comprehensive revision and improvement. www.3aicn.com

GB 23864-2009 "fire blocking material" national standards to retain the original content on proven industry standard in cooperation

Reasonably practicable the performance requirements and test methods, taking into account the EU's EN 1366-3, EN 1366-4 standard, combined with standard

Implementation experience, different fire protection and fire sealing system, sealing materials, the fire resistance test to do a more clear and reasonable requirements

Increase the durability and some physical and chemical properties of the technical requirements. www.3aicn.com

GB 23864-2009 "fire blocking material" national standard applies to buildings, structures and facilities of all kinds

All through the holes, cracks construction materials used in fire prevention or fire sealing components sealing also apply to all types of fire cover

Blocking materials and products formed in different ways to install the system, and fire protection and fire-resistant insulation materials, sealing material composed of fire

Block system and determine the fire resistance test. GB 23864-2009 "fire blocking material," including the national standard fire

Sealing material terms and definitions, classification and marking, requirements, test methods, inspection rules, comprehensive evaluation criteria and packaging,

Signs, storage, transportation and so on. Compared with the original industry standard, the new national standard in the scope and technical content are

Large expansion, better adapt to current and future technology development and engineering practical applications. www.3aicn.com
