To effectively create a "build cool all the Iron Fire, fire guards are striving to be cool," the ideological basis, the detachment will vigorously carry forward the spirit of new era of firefighting, to take effective measures to deepen the theme of education, strengthening the ideological and political work to create the Iron fire.
