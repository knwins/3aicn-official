Liupanshui fire brigade based on the actual needs, and actively build firefighting Iron innovative ideas and methods, in-depth implementation of the "five small five" training mode, focus on improving the quality of the city's fire brigade training, to ensure the city's fire brigade at the critical moment, "pull out , get into the attack, win the war. To carry out "little education", reinforce the big ideas. To effectively create a "build cool all the Iron Fire, fire guards are striving to be cool," the ideological basis, the detachment will vigorously carry forward the spirit of new era of firefighting, to take effective measures to deepen the theme of education, strengthening the ideological and political work to create the Iron fire. Detachment always adhere to the scientific concept of development as the guiding ideology of the building, in strict accordance with the relevant Ministry of Public Security and the Corps of party arrangements and requirements, carry out an extensive study of political education, organization focused on the officers and men to visit troops in team history, the theme of the film watching the Revolutionary War and brave deeds, and effectively cultivate the tenacious fighting style, from a real ideological establishment of a "conquering, invincible, bravery, fighting for victory," the Fire Iron Army team.

Change the "little way", mining a large potential. Detachment in order to improve soldiers' fight strong races, fires, looting big risk, save big disaster, "the ability as a fundamental goal, according to the warrior culture, physical, psychological and other characteristics of the scientific development of training standards, individualized instruction, step by step. And for training individual soldiers tired of psychology, rely on the active detachment party to go face to face, heart to heart communication, helping them to establish confidence in overcoming difficulties, reduce their psychological stress, to guide them out of difficulties, so that they easier to move forward, bravely catching up to ensure that duty training in a planned, step by step to push forward.

To carry out "little training" to enhance the large capacity. In recent years, along with fire fighting and rescue task difficulty increased gradually in order to ensure the officers and men in the face of various types of disasters can be handy when, in the training, the selected squad backbone, business equipment and equipment for all the top technical performance, functional use , operating procedures and maintenance methods to explain, and concrete steps, to explain security to the demonstration in order to achieve the best combination of people and equipment.
