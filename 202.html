3, on-site construction safety management

1) the construction process, construction workers and strictly implement "Production Safety Law," "electrical safety procedures," "power construction safety regulations," "to prevent major accidents electricity production twenty-five demands" " Construction Quality Control Regulations "and the relevant national laws and regulations, the industry standard and higher level requirements;

2) pre-construction, all construction workers to receive on-site safety education and proposed security precautions, all on-site construction must comply with the safety requirements to obey the command staff in the application and agreed before work can begin. All construction personnel only in designated activities within the scope of construction, can not enter other non-construction scope. Prohibited without authorization, cross (through) the more secure facility; www.3aicn.com

3) before the start of construction workers must be familiar with engineering design drawings, and in strict accordance with design drawings, installation instructions and operating instructions of quality standards of construction to ensure construction quality and design standard requirements. Found that the design drawings appear wrong, missing should be reported promptly to the construction unit to get clear answers before continuing construction. www.3aicn.com

4) construction workers into the construction site must wear a work permit, wear a helmet, put on overalls and insulated shoes. During the construction process, timely and responsible comrades to keep in touch, to prevent security risks (fire, electric shock, smooth lines, falling objects, mechanical damage, etc.) to keep the face clean, to prevent accidents due to scene clutter.

5) full-time security staff can not leave the construction site during the construction period, the whole process must be carefully tracked, and implement security measures. Person in charge of construction site work, work guardian, according to the provisions of wearing a full-time security staff responsible person (guardian) sign armband;

6) construction must be self-construction of the necessary construction equipment and qualified security tools and instruments are not allowed to use other facilities;

7) place of work should have adequate lighting.

8) The construction workers must pay attention to its own security and safety of electrical equipment to avoid accidents. Start a day before the responsible person must be accountable for working class people the day of the work content and safety precautions, and make a record;

9) work, we must ensure that at least two construction workers at the construction site simultaneously;

10) out of the power distribution room, hyperbaric chamber must apply for a work permit and shut the door is good;

11) prohibit the unauthorized installation in high-voltage power distribution room, control room digging holes, or remove the vermin shield;

12) thunderstorms prohibit outdoor venue work; www.3aicn.com

13) The construction workers are not allowed to touch the live electrical equipment. In the high-pressure field moving ladders, pipes and other belongings, they should be moving down to the ground, and with the live parts to maintain adequate safe distance. On energized equipment can not be less than 1.5 m distance (110kV) or 0.7 m (10kV);

14) construction workers in the electric equipment should be used around the foot wooden ruler and plastic (not metal clip) to measure work

15) construction personnel must use the hammer drill with good goggles;

16) Construction personnel shall not use the jigsaw in the room may be used only outdoors and away from the body charged to high voltage;

17) work at height to pass objects, not throw down;

Engineering Installation: 3MCS-195 + Composite fire board, the joints are solidified with fire clay and non-solidified block.

(1) the construction process:

Clean-up grassroots - Loft - hole - cut fire board - fireproof filing blunt edge of the board - the cable wrapped with fire retardant - fire fighting solidified mud - Fixed fire board - fill deposited non-solidified fire mud - check additional fire clay - aluminum foil tape paste - clean fire board - paste warning signs

(2) Operating points: 

 Clean-up grassroots: include cleaning, removal of concrete construction residue and loose parts 

 Loft: CS195 + composite board with the primary fire overlap length ≥ 5cm, according to the size and fire board size, playing the line to set the bolt holes, bolt axis spacing ≤ 15cm, divided equally on each side. And ensure that every corner has bolted, according to the well marked. 

 Hole: 6 ~ 8mm in diameter with the impact along the drill hole, install the bolts.

Cutting fire board: sheet metal cutting tools, according to site size cutting CS195 + composite fire board, is not damaged and did not cut some galvanized steel wire, aluminum foil and other materials, and be sure to install the test equipment requirements.

 Damage the cable. 

 Of the cable wrapped with fire retardant: flame arresters for cable and cable wrap belt.

 Fixed Fire Board: Fire board required to bolt at the grassroots level, the installation should ensure that galvanized steel galvanized side out.

2) engineering fire blocking material must have a product name, type, size, number, certificate and so on.

3) cable channel, cable tunnel fire block, separated, sealing holes in the cable through the wall signs should be clearly marked.

4) Fire sealing (fire clay) should allow the construction of the cable mounted together in one uniform, seamless between the cable. Fire blocking material to use for 10 years without dry powder phenomenon.

5) ring around the cable bundle clearance, fire-resistant sealing material should be tight closure.

6) with the fire board, fire clay, fire retardant fire retardant made with other wall, floor and wall cable through the hole blocking body, the fire board, fire clay, fire retardant cable with the other and should be combined with each other tight, solid sound. Have a certain ability to shock and vibration proof.

7) The fire board should be no gap after installation, cracks, smooth appearance and beautiful. Fire board around and through the annular gap between the cable should be used to block fire closed.
