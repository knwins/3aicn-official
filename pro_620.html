Fire mortar mixing guide

1. First added to the bucket of water, and then the plaster with an awl into the bucket (awl larger, into the slower the better).
Mixing ratio of 1 unit of water than 2.5 units (weight) of the plaster to 1 unit of water than 4 units of plaster. When the mortar filled with hand tools to the real wall openings, the ratio of 1:3.5 is more appropriate. Hardening time will affect the consistency, more water ratio (eg 1:2.8) in a long time to achieve sufficient coagulation.
2. To avoid the end of the proportional value. When water consumption is low, the corresponding fixtures use less. When the water is higher, will have a strong self-leveling concentration.
3. At least 5 minutes to mixing mortar evenly smooth. Note barrel at the bottom and around the dry plaster particles.
4. Do not second mixing

Note:
1) mortar fire in the wet state that can conduct electricity, so do not install the cable with exposed wires touch. When the situation is more complex, you can ask an electrical engineer.
2) When there are cracks on the wall or floor, it must be on crack and joint sealing systems set the displacement, or may extend to cracks in the system at the block. Can tap to determine the crack towards. When run through the material, it can be judged by the same way crack. When there are more serious cracks, the coating can be used to avoid the sticky end of the deficiencies.
3) fire clay mortar and CP25WB + can play with the use of dense smoke effect.
4) Do not apply the plaster on the active pipeline, usually insulated pipe will be activities, a 3mm thick quality paper to ceramic coated pipes, which can prevent cracks caused due to pipeline activities. Do not block out material coated on both sides.
5) This is not load-bearing mortar fire, please refer to product characteristics in the compressive strength. Existing specifications do not have a fire blocking material can be load-bearing requirements.
